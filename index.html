Who we are

What is the BCM? Well contary to some it does not stand for Bearded Cult Ministries. The Baptist Collegiette Ministries is a campus bible study. We are devoted to building a community of believers and helping them get connected locally within the school and externally with other churches and orginazations. We are a community of believers united together with the goal of serving Christ.
